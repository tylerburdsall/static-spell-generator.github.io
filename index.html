Import from D&D Beyond

Paste the URL of a D&D Beyond character (or just the numeric character ID). I'll pull every Cantrip and 1st-level spell the character knows.

The character sheet must be set to Public on D&D Beyond. On the character page, open the gear icon → Privacy and switch the visibility to Public. Otherwise this importer can't read it.

Importing replaces your current selection.
